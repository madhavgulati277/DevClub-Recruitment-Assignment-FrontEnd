About DevClub IITD

DevClub is a student group at IIT Delhi, where students apply their skills into developing applications for the benefit of the society, and enhencing their own skills in the process

Hey Folks!

DevClub, or the development club, is a student group working to promote industry-oriented software development at the institute. The members work on developing, maintaining and updating a host of student-oriented projects such as Citadel, the one-stop platform for past question papers and textbooks, and Portfolio Creator, an interactive tool to help students create their portfolio website.

The club organizes various events throughout the year, ranging from Hackathons and Capture the Flags to Lectures and talks by experts. DevClub has a dual motive of equipping students with industry-relevant skills as well as building software to fulfil the needs and requirements of the campus folk.

Hope you enjoy the Exciting Stuff :)

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the skills learnt in DevClub

Ans. Like in every club, DevClub also teaches many skills. The main ones include Software Development, Problem Solving and Teamwork

What is the Recruitment Process for DevClub

Ans. An assignment followed by an interview. Recruitments take place for both design and development roles.

When does the process of recruitment begin?

Ans. Usually in the second semester

What is the general location of club activities

Ans. Any place with enough space and charging sockets but usually Bharti building.

I'm quite interested in Web Development and related fields, but I think I'm not that proficient. What should I do?

Ans. Well, there are a series of workshops before recruitment. Videos of previous year workshops are there on DevClub IITD YouTube channel (see footer). Also, even after that you think you are not that proficient, you can join as an event executive or event coordinator, as you'll still get to be involved with tech enthusiasts.
